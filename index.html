About Party Planner

The Party Planner app provides an all-in-one solution to planning a party and all the details involved.

Time to party? It's time to PARTY PLANNER!

Plan It

Party Planner allows users to add a party, number of guests, the party theme, party date, party budget and an overall to-do list.

Shop It

Party Planner includes a shopping list with the ability to mark items off as purchased, subtracting them from the remaining budget

Photograph It

Add pictures to the party mood board. Shake it like a polaroid picture.
